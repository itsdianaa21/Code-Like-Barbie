Who is Barbie?

Barbie is many things!

Barbie is a fashion doll created by Ruth Handler, manufactured by the toy company Mattel, having launched back in 1959. I, like many others, went to see the movie that came out in 2023, and was amazed at how beautiful and touching it was. While not everyone had the same reaction nor got the same message from it, I believe it is something everyone could learn from.
